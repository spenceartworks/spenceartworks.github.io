SPENCE ARTWORKS

Good Old-fashioned Contemporary Art

Mr. Spence is a Scottish artist redefining pin-up for a contemporary era, celebrating beauty, strength, and vulnerability beyond the clichés of the past. Working in oil, watercolour, or pastel, his figures blend classic elegance with modern charm.

Beyond figurative work, he explores landscapes and shares his artistic journey on YouTube, bringing more variety and insight to his creative world.
