SPENCE ARTWORKS

Good Old-fashioned Contemporary Art

Mr. Spence is a Scottish artist redefining pin-up for a contemporary era, celebrating beauty, strength, and vulnerability beyond the clichés of the past. Working in oil, watercolour, or pastel, his figures blend classic elegance with modern charm.

Beyond figurative work, he explores landscapes and shares his artistic journey on YouTube, bringing more variety and insight to his creative world.

Graceful Adjustment

Corina carefully wraps a red ribbon around her stocking, exuding elegance and timeless charm.

Reclining Radiance

A stunning oil painting of Iya in a blue dress, lying gracefully against a cool-toned background.

Pensive in Red

A contemplative portrait of Nata sitting on the floor, her red attire contrasting with the warm lighting.

Playful Pin-Up

A classic pin-up pose, capturing the lighthearted yet sultry essence of vintage glamour.
